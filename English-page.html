About us

Introduction

We, ΔVerse(Deltaverse), is a group of game creators consisting from four high school members. We mainly use Unity and Blender. Since founded in the April of 2022, we have been working to make amusing games.
We made games to live up school festival, and we played the games we made in one hour with each other. And now, we are making the game for the Unity Cup comig up in 2023.
